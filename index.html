About Join the DOTS

Join The DOTS is emergent phenomenon. It is game-like, but resistant to categorisation.

It can be described as a global, chalk-based, wellbeing movement, or collaboration.

Purpose

Join the DOTS exists to return voice and agency to the street.

It aims to empower ordinary people and expand democracy.

Join The DOTS is ‘connective tissue’.

It seeks to integrate the countless ideas and demands for justice into a coherent whole capable of capturing and inspiring our individual and collective imaginations.

Rationale

Join the DOTS insists that the optical delusion of a 'self' defined by separateness from the rest of the universe simply does not cut it any more.

Everyday lifeworld events increasingly demonstrate that our fates are intertwined and our individual and collective wellbeing are ultimately inseparable.

Acknowledging this, it becomes apparent that our own personal wellbeing is integrally linked to, and dependent upon, our collective wellbeing.

From this perspective, dedication to a lifeworld defined by universal justice, compassion and care becomes a matter of enlightened self-interest.

This view is not so radical.

What is radical is our departure from this truth via a current cultural pattern of escalating extraction, exploitation, and atomisation.

Join the DOTS believes MWe - urgently need a new hegemony, or common sense.

And MWe consider 'MWe' a term that integrates and honours both the 'me' and the 'we' - a sufficient meme around which the new and transformative common-sense could coalesce.

Join the DOTS considers the arena of the self expansive enough, meaningful enough, and resonant enough, to contain all the myriad struggles for a just world.

Method

The medium is the message. Pavement chalk is both practically and symbolically optimal as a means of communication and to legitimately claim space.

How To Play

Like all the best games, the rules are simple but the plays are infinite.

  • Play safe. Play social.
  • Chalk with an open heart and mind.
  • Use the simplest of chalks. Pavement chalk is ideal.
  • Chalk openly. Claim your space.
  • Include a Unifying Meme
  • Whenever possible, leave some chalk behind. It's a conversation!

Unifying Memes

The Unifying memes are those symbols and phrases which, when inscribed in chalk, gently link any individual expression to the broader frame of Join the DOTS.

Unifying memes include:

  • the dotZero symbol
  • the phrase 'Join the DOTS'
  • the phrase 'Democracy On The Streets'
  • the word 'Eternity'
  • the word 'Infinity'
  • the pronoun 'MWe'
  • InfinityDOTS (two dots, side by side, different colours)

Sometime, somewhere, a single chalk dot might suffice.

Why should I connect to Join the DOTS?

While Join the DOTS is iterative and evolving, certain qualities are inherent in its DNA.

Join the DOTS:

  • asserts and celebrates our fundamental freedom expression.
  • seeks to expand democracy.
  • is concerned with connecting, honouring and integrating the 'Me' and the 'We'.
  • is non-violent.
  • is dialogic.
  • is rooted in community.
  • is inclusive.
  • celebrates free speech, creativity and imagination.
  • encourages outreach, connection and transformation.

If these values resonate with you, pick up chalk and Join the DOTS.

MWe need you. Really.

Who are MWe?

Join The DOTS coalesced in the mind of a single individual (me) during a period of COVID lockdown.

The moment of epiphany was realising DOTS was an acronym for Democracy On The Streets.

This realisation has proven wildly generative, rewarding - and a little weird - ever since.

While Join The DOTS surfaced in an individual mind, it is informed by a host of disparate interactions and perspectives.

William Gibson might call it a ‘Branch Plant of MWe’.

In any case, it is appropriate to say it is authored by MWe.

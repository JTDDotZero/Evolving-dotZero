About Join the DOTS

Join The DOTS is emergent phenomenon. It is game-like, but resistant to categorisation.

It can be described as a global, chalk-based, wellbeing movement, or collaboration.

Purpose

Join the DOTS exists to return voice and agency to the street.

It aims to empower ordinary people and expand democracy.

Ultimately, Join The DOTS seeks to serve as ‘connective tissue’.

It works to integrate superficially disparate ideas and causes into a coherent whole, capable of capturing and inspiring our individual and collective imaginations.

Rationale

Join the DOTS insists that the optical delusion of a 'self' defined by separateness from the rest of the universe simply does not cut it any more.

It is now obvious that our own personal wellbeing is delimited by the wellbeing of the world we are a part of.

Acknowledging this, dedication to improving the welfare of others and of the planet becomes a matter of enlightened self-interest.

This view is not so radical.

What is radical is our departure from this truth via a current cultural pattern of escalating extraction, exploitation, and atomisation.

The dominant political and cultural values of past decades no longer serve.

Join the DOTS believes MWe urgently need a new hegemony , or common sense.

And MWe consider the arena of the self expansive enough, meaningful enough, and resonant enough, to contain all the myriad struggles for a just world.

We put forward 'MWe' - a term that integrates and honours both the 'me' and the 'we' - as a sufficient meme around which the new and transformative common-sense can coalesce.

Method

The medium is the message. Pavement chalk is both practically and symbolically optimal as a means of communication and to legitimately claim space.

How To Play

Put simply, Join The DOTS is about putting chalk to pavement and saying what's on your mind.

It is about 'learning by doing' and makes the most sense when practiced.

  • Play safe. Play social.
  • Chalk with an open heart and mind.
  • Use the simplest of chalks. Pavement chalk is ideal.
  • Chalk openly. Claim your space.
  • Include a Unifying Meme
  • Whenever possible, leave some chalk behind. It's a conversation!

    • Unifying Memes

    The Unifying memes are those symbols and phrases which, when inscribed in chalk, gently link any individual expression to the broader frame of Join the DOTS.

    Unifying memes include:

    • the dotZero symbol
    • the phrase 'Join the DOTS'
    • the phrase 'Democracy On The Streets'
    • the word 'Eternity'
    • the word 'Infinity'
    • the pronoun 'MWe'
    • InfinityDOTS (two dots, side by side, different colours)

    Sometime, somewhere, a single chalk dot might suffice.

    Long Game Memes

    Long game memes are where alchemy and creativity kick in.

    There are no specific 'rules'.

    Long Game Memes can be anything from rainbows to rants, scribblings to slogans

    Their overall strategic purpose is to encourage participation, expand the democractic space and prompt, promote and provoke new thinking.

    Long Game Memes are what ensure the ideas lying around belong to the community.

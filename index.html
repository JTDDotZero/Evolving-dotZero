About Join the DOTS

Join The DOTS is emergent phenomenon. It is game-like, but resistant to categorisation.

It can be described as a global, chalk-based, wellbeing movement – or collaboration.

Purpose

Join the DOTS exists to expand available space for human voice and agency; dialogue; imagination; and, a fuller sense of ‘the self’.

In particular, Join The DOTS seeks to provide ‘connective tissue’: to link the myriad actions to realise a more just, sustainable and satisfactory way of 'being' in the world, through an expanded sense of self and a subsequent shared perception of ‘enlightened self-interest’.

Rationale

Join the DOTS believes that the optical delusion of the self as separate from the rest of the universe simply does not cut it any more.

It is now obvious and inescapable that our fates are intertwined and our individual and collective wellbeing are inseparable.

From this it follows that a more just and equitable world is a matter of urgent self-interest for everybody.

Join the DOTS believes that this universal frame – of ultimate self-interest – is expansive enough, meaningful enough, and resonant enough, to encapsulate and connect all the myriad impulses to justice currently swirling throughout our world.

MWe believe cultivating such a frame to connect all our efforts is both appropriate and essential to establishing a new common sense, or hegemony, capable of uniting and inspiring humanity to respond appropriately to our profound and existential challenges.

Method

The medium is the message. Pavement chalk is both practically and symbolically optimal as a means to communicate and legitimately claim space.

How To Play

Like all the best games, the rules are simple but the plays are infinite.

  • Play safe. Play social.
  • Chalk with an open heart and mind.
  • Use the simplest of chalks. Pavement chalk is ideal.
  • Chalk openly. Claim your space.
  • Include a Unifying Meme
  • Whenever possible, leave some chalk behind. It's a conversation!

Unifying Memes

The Unifying memes are those symbols and phrases which, when inscribed in chalk, gently link contextmenu=""any individual expression to the broader frame of Join the DOTS.

Unifying memes include:

  • the dotZero symbol
  • the phrase 'Join the DOTS'
  • the phrase 'Democracy On The Streets'
  • the word 'Eternity'
  • the word 'infinity'
  • the pronound 'MWe'
  • InfinityDOTS (two dots, side by side, different colours)

Sometime, somewhere, a single chalk dot might suffice.

Why should I connect to Join the DOTS?

While Join the DOTS is iterative, changeable and learning as it goes, certain values are fixed deep into its DNA. These values speak to the self, non-violence, free expression, community, democracy, enquiry, outreach, connection and transformation.

If these values resonate with you and describe the world you seek to realise, please Join the DOTS.

MWe need you. Really.

Who are MWe?

Join The DOTS coalesced in the mind of a single individual (me) during a period of COVID lockdown.

The moment of epiphany was realising DOTS was an acronym for Democracy On The Streets.

This realisation opened the floodgates and the concept has been wildly generative, intrinsically rewarding, and kinda weird, ever since.

While Join The DOTS did indeed surface in my individual mind, it is informed by a host of disparate experiences and perspectives. William Gibson might consider it the product of a ‘Branch Plant of MWe’.

In any case, it is appropriate to say it belongs to, and is being authored by, MWe.

MWe invite and encourage you to become a party to its further realisation.

Pich up that chalk!

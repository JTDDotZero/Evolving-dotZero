About Join the DOTS

Join The DOTS is emergent phenomenon. It is game-like, but resistant to categorisation.

It can be described as a global, chalk-based, wellbeing movement, or collaboration.

Purpose

Join the DOTS seeks to expand available space for communication, expression and a reimagining of the self.

In particular, Join The DOTS exists to provide ‘connective tissue’.

It offers a meaningful and inclusive framework for integrating the countless ways in which we each demand and create a more just, sustainable and satisfactory world.

Rationale

Join the DOTS recognises that the optical delusion of a separated self, cut off from the rest of the universe, simply is not cutting it any more.

Real world events demonstrate daily that our fates are intertwined and our individual and collective wellbeing are ultimately inseparable.

Acknowledging this, it becomes apparaent our own personal wellbeing is dependent on global wellbeing.

A world defined by justice, compassion and respect for 'others' becomes a matter of enlightened self-interest.

Join the DOTS believes that the frame of the self is expansive enough, meaningful enough, and resonant enough, to encompass all the myriad demands for a just world.

MWe believe a view of the self as 'Mwe', which balances and respects both the 'me' and the 'we' - will provides the necessary 'germ' around which a new common-sense, or hegemony can rapidly coalesce.

Method

The medium is the message. Pavement chalk is both practically and symbolically optimal as a means of communication and to legitimately claim space.

How To Play

Like all the best games, the rules are simple but the plays are infinite.

  • Play safe. Play social.
  • Chalk with an open heart and mind.
  • Use the simplest of chalks. Pavement chalk is ideal.
  • Chalk openly. Claim your space.
  • Include a Unifying Meme
  • Whenever possible, leave some chalk behind. It's a conversation!

Unifying Memes

The Unifying memes are those symbols and phrases which, when inscribed in chalk, gently link any individual expression to the broader frame of Join the DOTS.

Unifying memes include:

  • the dotZero symbol
  • the phrase 'Join the DOTS'
  • the phrase 'Democracy On The Streets'
  • the word 'Eternity'
  • the word 'Infinity'
  • the pronoun 'MWe'
  • InfinityDOTS (two dots, side by side, different colours)

Sometime, somewhere, a single chalk dot might suffice.

Why should I connect to Join the DOTS?

While Join the DOTS is iterative and will evolve, certain values and goals are fundamentally encoded into its base DNA.

Join the DOTS:

  • asserts a universal right to the 'creative commons'
  • seeks to expand democratic space.
  • is concerned with connecting, honouring and integrating the 'Me' and the 'We'.
  • uses chalk only (is non-violent).
  • is dialogic.
  • is rooted in community.
  • is inclusive.
  • celebrates free speech, creativity and imagination.
  • encourages outreach, connection and transformation.

If these values resonate with you, MWe invite to pick up chalk and Join the DOTS.

MWe need you. Really.

Who are MWe?

Join The DOTS coalesced in the mind of a single individual (me) during a period of COVID lockdown.

The moment of epiphany was realising DOTS was an acronym for Democracy On The Streets.

This realisation opened the floodgates and the concept has been wildly generative, intrinsically rewarding, and kinda weird, ever since.

While Join The DOTS surfaced in an individual mind, it is informed by a host of disparate interactions and perspectives.

William Gibson might call it a ‘Branch Plant of MWe’.

In any case, it is appropriate to say it belongs to, and is authored by, MWe.
